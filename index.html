steps to the project

How we
Do It

SURVEYING

Reconnaissance Survey was conducted at the site.
Site was clearly investigated and a reference sketch of the ground was prepared, also the principal features like buildings, roads, etc. are noted.

The detailed survey was carried out using tape.
Boundary points were fixed and area of the site was measured. The measurements taken by tape survey and the total area of the site = 6500 m2

FORMA

Through these sophisticated analyses facilitated by Autodesk Forma, the project gained valuable insights, ensuring that the energy-efficient educational building will be strategically positioned and designed to maximize sustainability while harmonizing with its natural surroundings.

The visualization of daylight potential, in harmony with the surrounding buildings and environment, provided a nuanced understanding of the site's lighting dynamics. The assessment of rooftop solar energy potential, particularly focusing on photovoltaic panel systems, highlighted opportunities for harnessing renewable energy..

REVIT & AUTOCAD

Through AutoCAD, the basic layout and initial design concepts were crafted.
The crafted sketch was then imported in Revit for further designing.

Elaborated designs of the institution were created using Revit.
Revit facilitated the creation of a comprehensive model and a detailed plan that  integrate sustainable elements such as optimal spatial arrangements, natural  lighting, and efficient ventilation systems.

ETABs

The focus of the structural design phase is to ensure the building's strength while aligning with energy-efficient principles using ETABS. 

It involves detailed planning of material utilization, structural components, and load-bearing elements to amplify energy efficiency. 

Green building Studio

Green Building Studio utilizes simulations and analysis to assess factors like energy usage, water consumption, and carbon emissions, helping designers optimize sustainability and efficiency in their projects.

Simulations identify areas of potential energy loss, providing critical insights for optimization. Simulation results are then analyzed to recommend design modifications aimed at significantly enhancing the building's overall energy efficiency.

revit

Using Revit we delve into a detailed breakdown of expenses related to energy-efficient features and strategies. Analyze meticulously to ensure that the project aligns with the allocated budget while following current PWD rates.

The assessment helps demonstrate the feasibility and long-term sustainability of the proposed design, extending beyond just energy efficiency.

twinmotion

Develop detailed 3D visual representations and renderings of the proposed design using Twinmotion. Twinmotion is a real-time visualization and 3D immersion software designed for architecture, urban planning, and landscaping. 

The visualizations will offer a clear depiction of how the building design comply with energy-efficient principles. This phase will aid in communicating design aspects, facilitating a clearer vision and understanding of the building's potential upon completion.
